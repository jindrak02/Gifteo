Features:

    Gift ideas for your loved ones
    Save and organize gift ideas for people in your life — even if they don’t use Gifteo.

    Wishlist sharing
    Cooperate with others on your close ones wishlists. Manage who buys what and discuss about it.

    Links to buy gifts
    Add links to your wishlists so that whoever wants to buy you the gift knows exactly where.

    Flexible sharing options
    Share wishlists with specific people or your entire trusted circle (“My People”).

    Smart reminders
    Gifteo notifies you about upcoming birthdays, holidays, and special occasions — never forget an important date again.

    Your gift dashboard
    Instantly view important events, gift ideas, and wishlists for everyone who matters to you.
